What it does

Built for freelancers who hate chasing invoices

Most invoice spreadsheets start clean and go messy fast. This one doesn't. Every row is an invoice: client, amount, due date, status. Totals and overdue counts update automatically as you mark invoices paid.

The formula tab shows every calculation in plain language so you understand what's happening — no black-box functions, no locked cells.

Sample data comes pre-filled so you can see exactly how the tracker works before you clear it and make it yours. Takes about five minutes to set up for real.

  • ✓Auto-calculates total billed, total paid, and outstanding balance
  • ✓Flags overdue invoices automatically based on due date
  • ✓Tracks multiple clients in one sheet with per-client subtotals
  • ✓Currency-agnostic — works in any currency, any locale
  • ✓Monthly income summary tab for tax time
  • ✓No scripts, no add-ons, no Google Workspace plan needed
